Work profile setup




How to work profile Google account to your Android phone

Follow the steps

1. Launch Settings from your Home screen, the app drawer.

2. Swipe up in the Settings menu to scroll down

3. Tap Accounts.

4. Tap Add Account.

5. Tap Google.

6. Type your company email id. (ex: vishnu@rdskytech.com)

7. Tap Next.

8. Type your password.

9. Tap Next.

10. Update Android policy.

11. Tap next upto work profile setp complete message.

12. Open work profile Gmail.

View/Download setup PDF

13. Click on the slack invitation email and set slack password .

14. Download slack.

15. Open Gmail and click on the ZOHO CRM invitation link.

16. Download zoho through invitation link only.

17. Open playstore and download rdsky field ops app.

Welldone.. you all done.
